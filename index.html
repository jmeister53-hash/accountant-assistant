Accountant Assistant

A private bookkeeping helper, run locally by the business owner.

Review and correct your own QuickBooks Online books

Accountant Assistant is a private tool that the business owner runs on their own computer. It connects to the owner's own QuickBooks Online companies to review the books and help find and fix mistakes.

What it is

This is a personal, single-operator tool. It is not sold, distributed, or made available to anyone else. One person — the business owner — installs it on their own Windows computer and uses it only with their own QuickBooks Online companies (two cleaning businesses).

How it works

  • It connects to QuickBooks Online through Intuit's official, authorized connection, using the owner's own login approval.
  • By default it is read-only — it looks at the books (accounts, transactions, and reports) to review them, but changes nothing.
  • The only changes it makes are corrections the owner reviews and approves one at a time. Nothing is changed automatically or in bulk.
  • Everything runs and is stored locally on the owner's own computer. No data is sent to any outside service.

Connect or reconnect

Connecting and reconnecting to QuickBooks Online happens inside the tool on the owner's own computer. When the owner starts the tool, it opens Intuit's official sign-in screen so the owner can approve the connection. This page serves as the public home and connect/reconnect reference for that process.
