Accountant Assistant

A private bookkeeping helper, run locally by the business owner.

How to disconnect

You can disconnect Accountant Assistant at any time. There are two parts: turn off the connection inside QuickBooks Online, then remove the local data on your own computer.

Step 1 — Disconnect inside QuickBooks Online

  1. Sign in to QuickBooks Online.
  2. Open the Settings menu (the gear icon, top right).
  3. Choose Manage Apps (you can also do this from your account at intuit.com).
  4. Find Accountant Assistant in the list of connected apps.
  5. Select Disconnect and confirm.

This tells Intuit to stop allowing the tool to reach your books. After this, the saved connection keys on your computer no longer work.

Step 2 — Remove the local data on your computer

Everything the tool stored lives only on your own computer. To remove it:

  1. Delete the .env file in the tool's folder. This holds the connection credentials.
  2. Delete the data/ folder in the tool's folder. This holds the copies of accounting information the tool reviewed.

Once both are deleted, no QuickBooks credentials or accounting data remain on the computer.

That's it — the connection is off in QuickBooks Online and the local copies are gone. If you want to use the tool again later, you can reconnect from the home page and approve access through Intuit's sign-in screen.
